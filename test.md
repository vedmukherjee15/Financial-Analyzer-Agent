## What to Monitor (next 2–4 quarters)
To stay aligned with the 2–3 year thesis, watch:
- **Profitability trend**
  - ETERNAL: continued positive EBITDA/operating margin and improvement in cash generation
  - SWIGGY: narrowing losses and movement toward EBITDA breakeven
- **Cash flow trend**
  - Whether free cash flow losses shrink over time
- **Quick-commerce economics**
  - Signs that growth is becoming less promo/discount dependent

- ETERNAL’s profitability deteriorates (margins flip negative / repeated earnings miss)
- SWIGGY losses widen further and operational metrics don’t improve for multiple quarters
